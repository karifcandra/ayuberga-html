About Us

Who We Are

Ayuberga is a digital platform specializes in providing personalized Umrah pilgrimage packages. Established in 2017 and headquartered in Dubai, our mission is to make your Umrah journey accessible, affordable, and transformative for pilgrims across the globe. We also offer an extended service to those who want to go beyond Umrah and explore the country.

It's not just about booking a trip - it's about promising an unforgettable experience. Our core belief - "Let Us Take You There!" - is not just an offer, but a commitment to make every moment of your journey unforgettable.

Our Story

Recognizing the limitations of traditional tour operators who offered rigid, fixed packages, Ayuberga stepped forward to revolutionize the industry. We've created a dedicated digital platform that enables customizable Umrah and Umrah Plus Packages.

Our mission goes beyond tickets or booking services; we strive to transform the pilgrimage of Umrah into a well-guided, spiritually uplifting journey, catering to the individual needs and preferences of the pilgrims.

We are dedicated to assisting every pilgrim through each phase of their Umrah, from the planning stage to performing the sacred rituals and extending our support well beyond the pilgrimage itself. We are firm in our commitment to

ensuring a deeply meaningful, tranquil experience, and a clear vision to revolutionize the Umrah experience using advanced technology.

Our Core Values

With Ayuberga, you're never alone. Be it resolving minor queries or managing crucial aspects of Umrah, our dedicated team stands by your side, ensuring a hassle-free experience

Our digital platform and community, #UmrahwithMe, symbolize more than just a service; it's a testament to our commitment to bringing Muslims together, fostering deep bonds, and sharing experiences. With a wide array of services specifically designed to cater to your requirements, we steadfastly meet our commitment to making Umrah accessible, affordable, and profoundly transformational.

  1. Guidance at every step
  2. Enriching experience
  3. 24/7 support
  4. Innovative Excellence

Our Technological Edge

Ayuberga stands as a pioneer in offering customizable online Umrah packages, including integrated real-time booking for flights, accommodation, visas, and more, all supported by our advanced technology and 3rd party service providers.

Why choose Ayuberga?

In today's fast-paced world, we understand the challenges faced by many who yearn for this spiritual journey: time constraints, visa complications, and post-booking confusion. We've seamlessly integrated solutions for these challenges, ensuring a smooth and memorable journey from the planning phase to the moment you return home.

We offer customized packages tailored to fit your distinct needs. In addition to the pilgrimage, our itineraries also include cultural explorations to destinations like Riyadh, Jeddah, Abha, Al Ula and more.

Choosing us means you'll have a 24/7 dedicated support team always available, making your journey seamless.

Community Beyond Boundaries

We believe in the power of shared experience.

Our online community, #UmrahwithMe, fosters interaction between pilgrims, both experienced and newcomers, thus nurturing a supportive community that encourages guidance, exchange of valuable insights, and building lasting relationships.

  • Serving the Umrah ecosystem diligently for over six years
  • Industry-wide recognition for our technology and services
